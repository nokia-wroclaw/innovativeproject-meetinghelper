MeetingHelper


 MeetingHelper is a result of a team programming course realized by students at Wroclaw University of Technology, Faculty of Electronics. It was supervised by dr Marek Piasecki and Nokia company.

Project was presented at Wrocław University of Technology on Team Projects Conference (Konferencja Projektów Zespołowych) which was held on 23rd June 2014.
